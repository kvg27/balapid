Results of Race 1 JuniorGP Catalunya: Rueda Superior, Fadillah Arbi scattered


Jose Antonio Rueda won a convincing victory in Race 1 JuniorGP Catalunya through a dominant performance from the first lap. Meanwhile, Indonesian rider Fadillah Arbi Aditama was scattered behind.

I Gede Ardy Estrada, 12 May 2022 17.16

Team Estrella Galicia-Honda racer Jose Antonio Rueda was able to show a perfect performance as shown by Fabio Quartararo in the Catalunya MotoGP last weekend.


El Diablo at that time succeeded in winning the race through a dominant appearance, finishing with an advantage of more than 6 seconds over P2. The same thing was shown by Rueda on the same track, Sunday (12/6/2022).


The young Spaniard finished Race 1 JuniorGP at Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya with a lead of more than 11 seconds. His speed really can't be matched by his rivals.


Meanwhile, the second position was claimed by Rueda's teammate, Syafruddin Azman, after a fierce battle with three riders : Filippo Fairoli (P3), Angel Piqueras (P4) and David Salvador (P5).


Rueda got off to a good start. Starting from pole, he was able to keep the position. The racer was under pressure from Salvador. But it did not last long because the competitor actually lost his position.


Rueda finished the first lap with a 0.211 second lead over Azman. As the race progressed, he continued to widen the distance. Meanwhile, the competition for the runner-up position was fierce.


Salvador was able to move up to third after overtaking Azman in a straight sector towards Turn 1. He is now hunting for Piqueras. After a while, he managed to take over P2.


However, the fight didn't stop there. Azman as well as Piqueras immediately responded and managed to overtake Salvador. The fierce duel behind him made Rueda even more comfortable in front.


Entering the last 10 laps, Rueda was even more superior, he widened the gap to more than 5 seconds from Azman, who was still struggling to face the pressure of Salvador and Piqueras for P2.


On the other hand, Philip Tonn (Intact Junior GP) and Mattia Volpi (AGR Team) crashed at the last corner of the seventh lap. Both had to be eliminated from the race , but fortunately there was no injury.


Entering the second half of the race, Rueda was already about 7.5 seconds ahead of Piqueras, who was already able to take over the second position. The battle for the podium was even more intense after Farioli joined.


In the last five laps, the Italian managed to take advantage of the slipstream in the start/finish straight sector to overtake Salvador, Azman and Piqueras. Now Farioli occupies P2. But the competition continues.


The next lap Azman took over. Then two rounds remaining, Piqueras filled second position. In the final lap, Farioli took the lead, but Azman finally managed to win P2 from his rival.


Meanwhile, Indonesian representative Fadillah Arbi Aditama was only able to finish Race 1 in 28th place. Starting from P20, the Astra Honda Racing Team (AHRT) rider rose to 11th position .


However, unfortunately the opportunity to gain points failed to be maintained by Fadillah Arbi. Slowly his position slumped. There is still a chance for him to try in Race 2 JuniorGP Catalunya.
