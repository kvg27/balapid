First Points Score, Fadillah Arbi is More Comfortable


Indonesian rider Fadillah Arbi Aditama emphasized that he was more confident in playing the 2022 FIM JuniorGP after scoring his first points.

Muhamad Fadli Ramadan, 23 May 2022 14.00

The young racer fostered by PT Astra Honda Motor (AHM) Fadillah Arbi Aditama won his first point thanks to finishing 15th in the second race (Race 2) at the Ricardo Tormo Circuit, Sunday (22/5/2022).


Starting from the 20th grid of the Valencia JuniorGP, Arbi successfully took over several positions . At the start of the race, the young talent from Purworejo, Central Java was involved in an incident with another rider that sent him wide at Turn 11.


However, the racer who strengthened the Astra Honda Racing Team (AHRT) was able to recover his position quickly. He continued to show hard work in defending his position, and managed to win an important point.


"In the second race, my start wasn't too bad either. I was trying to stay in the second group, but another rider nudged me and I went wide again," said Arbi.


“I tried to get back up and finally got my first point of the season. I feel more and more comfortable with the bike and hopefully in the next races I can make progress again.”


In the first race (Race 1), Arbi almost won points by finishing 16th and only less than a tenth of a second adrift of 15th position.


Starting from 18th position, Arbi had collided with his rival and could not maintain his position. Although he managed to get back up on the last laps, he has not managed to get points.


This result is a motivation for Fadillah Arbi Aditama, especially this year is his first season competing in Europe at the junior level world championship.


GM Marketing Planning & Analysis AHM Andy Wijaya appreciated Arbi's fighting spirit who won the first points of this year's racing season.


Despite his minimal experience of racing on world circuits, racing with other great racers from all over the world, Arbi's struggle shows positive developments.


"In fostering racers to get the best performance and be able to compete on the world stage, we at PT Astra Honda Motor believe in the process," said Andy.


"Arbi shows this and we hope that he will continue to consistently improve his performance and make Indonesia proud."


The next series of the FIM JuniorGP will take place on 12 June 2022 at the Barcelona Circuit, with two races scheduled.


Fadillah Arbi Aditama is expected to be able to achieve the best results by bringing out his best abilities throughout the race.
