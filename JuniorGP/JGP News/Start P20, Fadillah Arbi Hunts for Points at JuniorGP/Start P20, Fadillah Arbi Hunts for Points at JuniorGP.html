Fadillah Arbi Ready to Learn Lessons at FIM JuniorGP

After completing his debut at the JuniorGP Estoril, Arbi left for Valencia for the second round of the season. Compared to the appearance during the opening series, there is a significant increase in performance.


Arbi opened the Valencia JuniorGP weekend by occupying the 16th position after the FP1 session. The best time of 1 minute 41.687 seconds. When completing FP2, the racer occupies P19.


After being unable to improve the lap time during the second free practice, Arbi was able to increase the lap time to 1 minute 41.514 seconds in the first qualifying (Q1) for 20th place.


But unfortunately, the Astra Honda Racing Team racers have difficulty continuing to improve. Scoring 1 minute 42.037 seconds, he finished Q2 in 17th position.


Although it didn't feel good, Arbi used the second qualification to run longer to get used to the race pace . This is also helped thanks to the much higher temperature.


Based on the results of the combination of Q1 and Q2, Arbi is in the 10th starting grid . The best time he took was in the first qualifying, Saturday (21/5/2022).


"In Q1, on the first run , I had a pretty good lap, and then I tried to improve, but there was so much traffic on the track that I lost time," he said, citing a press release.

The pole position was taken by Estrella Galicia 0.0 racer, namely Jose Antonio Rueda who overcame Luca Lunetta (AC Racing Team) and David Salvador (Laglisse Academy).


The Malaysian representative, Syarifuddin Azman, secured the fifth position. While the leader of the standings, Tatchakorn Buasri from Honda Racing Thailand, occupies the ninth grid .


The FIM JuniorGP Valencia will take place in two races. Race 1 starts at 16.00 WIB, and Race 2 is scheduled at 19.00 WIB on Sunday (22/5/2022).


Both competitions can be watched through the JuniorGP channel on YouTube .
