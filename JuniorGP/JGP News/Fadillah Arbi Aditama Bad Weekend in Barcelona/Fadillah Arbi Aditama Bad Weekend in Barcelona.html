Fadillah Arbi Aditama Bad Weekend in Barcelona


Astra Honda Racing Team racer, Fadillah Arbi Aditama, had to go home disappointed after completing the third series of JuniorGP Catalunya, Sunday (12/6/2022).

Scherazade Mulia Saraswati, 13 June 2022 15.30

Coming to the Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya, Arbi hopes to continue his positive streak after the success of gaining one point in the JuniorGP World Championship round in Valencia last year.


However, the two competitions in Barcelona did not go easy for the young man from Purworejo, Central Java. Starting from the sixth row, the racer could not avoid the incident involving him.


In Race 1, Arbi quickly penetrated the second group. With eight laps remaining, he was battling to secure his first top 10 finish of the season. Too bad for him. Other racers forced him off the track.


Even though he was able to survive, the young rider fostered by PT Astra Honda Motor (AHM) finally had to settle for finishing 28th. Meanwhile, Jose Antonio Rueda won the competition, who beat Syarifuddin Azman.


In Race 2, Arbi made a good start again. Overtake a number of rivals and prepare to enter the fight in the second group.


However, he was again unlucky. In the last nine, the Indonesian racer made contact with other racers. As a result of the incident, Arbi also recorded Did Not Finish (DNF).


“I (actually) feel comfortable this weekend. We improved from Valencia and Estoril, so that's the most important thing," he said in a written statement.


“In Race 1, I made a good start and I tried to find opportunities to overtake. After 10 laps, a rider in front of me hit the brakes early, so I had to go wide on the grass.


"In the second race, I tried to get a better feeling , because the timing was slower. I was in the second group, but unfortunately I hit another rider and fell.


"It's a race, but I'm happy with the work we did and will try even harder in the next laps."


Fadillah Arbi Aditama is in 25th place in the 2022 JuniorGP World Championship standings. Currently, he already has one point thanks to his 15th place finish in Valencia.


The fourth series of JuniorGP will be held at the Circuito de Jerez-Angel Nieto, Spain on July 3, early next month.
