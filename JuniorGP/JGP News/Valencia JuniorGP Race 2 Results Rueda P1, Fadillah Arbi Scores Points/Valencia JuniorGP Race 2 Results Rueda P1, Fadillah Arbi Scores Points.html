Valencia JuniorGP Race 2 Results: Rueda P1, Fadillah Arbi Scores Points


Jose Antonio Rueda successfully won in Race 2 JuniorGP Valencia, Sunday (22/5/2022). However, all the eyes of Indonesian racing fans are on Fadillah Arbi Aditama, who successfully scored points this time.

A neat start was shown by the racers in Race 2 JuniorGP Valencia, Sunday (22/5/2022). Team Estrella Galicia 0.0 racer, Jose Antonio Rueda, immediately took the lead as he entered Turn 1 of the Ricardo Tormo Circuit.


The second lap, there was an incident involving two riders at Turn 6. Jakob Rosenthaler (Liqui Moly Intact Junior GP Team) lost control of his motorbike and suffered a highside while exiting the corner.


Alex Gourdon (Leopard Impala Junior Team) who was right behind him couldn't dodge and ended up falling too. Luckily both riders were not injured.


The third lap, Soma Gorbe (Artbox) suffered a lowside during cornering. He is in good condition even though he was thrown to the side of the track.


The fifth lap, the change of race leader occurred. David Alonso had time to overtake the two drivers in front of him, Rueda and Angel Piqueras. However, the Estrella Galicia duo quickly took over again.


Still on the same lap, Rueda and Piqueras' teammate, Facundo Llambias, had a pretty terrible highside . Luckily he was not run over by a racer behind him.


Unfortunately, Llambias had to be carried off the track. It did look like it was crawling to the side just after the crash .


Lap 11, David Alonso again maneuvered at Turn 1. Again he overtook two drivers at once to take over P1.


But this time it was different, because the Aspar Junior Team racer managed to maintain his position. Rueda, Piqueras and Tatchakorn Buasri followed behind him.


Approaching the final lap, Buasri and Marcos Uriarte fell. Buasri fell first, causing Uriarte to run over his motorbike and crash into the gravel .


David Alonso's chances of winning ran aground after he suffered a lowside at the last corner. In the end, nothing prevented Rueda from taking Race 2 JuniorGP Valencia.


Filippo Farioli took second place, followed by David Salvador who completed the podium.


Meanwhile, the Indonesian racer, Fadillah Arbi Aditama, successfully finished in the points zone. Finishing in P15, Aditama finished Race 2 one position better than in Race 1.
