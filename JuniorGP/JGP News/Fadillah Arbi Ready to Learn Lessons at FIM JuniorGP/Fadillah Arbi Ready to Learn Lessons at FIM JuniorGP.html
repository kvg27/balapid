Fadillah Arbi Ready to Learn Lessons at FIM JuniorGP


Mainstay of Astra Honda Racing Team (AHRT) Fadillah Arbi Aditama will make his FIM JuniorGP debut and is ready to take many lessons there.

One of the most prestigious racing events in the world, namely the FIM JuniorGP, which is the development of young racers, was held again this year.


The young talent of Astra Honda Racing Team (AHRT) Fadillah Arbi will make his first appearance competing with 35 international racers in pursuit of the highest achievement for the good name of the nation in the international racing arena.


The Estoril Circuit, Portugal, which once hosted the MotoGP race, will be the venue for the first series of the 2022 FIM JuniorGP, on Sunday (8/5/2022).


The race on the European continent, previously known as CEV, has been consistently followed by AHRT racers for eight consecutive seasons, in order to develop the potential of young Indonesian talents to go to MotoGP.


Fadillah Arbi Aditama from Purworejo, Central Java is an alumnus of Astra Honda Racing School (AHRS) from 2017 to 2019. Prior to joining FIM JuniorGP, this 2005-born racer competed in the Asia Talent Cup championship in 2021.


Since the end of March 2022, Arbi has prepared himself by participating in a special training program for both physical and racing skills that has been prepared to provide maximum performance in the FIM JuniorGP event.


"This is certainly not an easy thing. I have been determined to be able to perform optimally and of course take every lesson in every race that I do,” said Arbi.


"Please pray and support me so that I can perform optimally and go through the initial process in Europe well and become a good starting capital to move forward."


General Manager Marketing Planning and Analysis AHM Andy Wijaya said the FIM JuniorGP racing event was one of the steps to continue to support and assist young Indonesian racers to make achievements in the international arena.


“The FIM JuniorGP event is one of our grading programs for AHRT racers to appear in world racing events. We hope that they will become the racing talents of Indonesia's young generation who will make the nation proud," said Andy.


FIM JuniorGP is a level of racing coaching carried out by AHM before moving on to the grand prix (GP) event.


This is evidenced by the actions of AHRT racers Dimas Ekky Pratama, Andy Gilang, Gerry Salim and Mario Suryo Aji in Moto2 and Moto3.


The four racers take turns representing Indonesia who have been in the world championships from 2019 until this year.


The opening round of the FIM JuniorGP 2022 will be held in one race on Sunday (8/5/2022) at 19:00 WIB. The race can be watched live on YouTube .
