SAG Team Gets Alex Toledo, Dimas Ekky Waits For Confirmation


Alex Toledo will be Piotr Biesiekirski's teammate in the 2022 JuniorGP Moto2. Meanwhile, the Indonesian racer, Dimas Ekky, is not yet known.

Scherazade Mulia Saraswati, 29 June 2022 20.00

Facing a new era of the FIM JuniorGP World Championship this season, SAG Racing Team has introduced a new duo. Biesiekirski is paired with Toledo.


In his first year at FIM CEV (before changing to JuniorGP), Biesiekirski managed to record two top five finishes. He also finished the championship in 13th place in the final standings, collecting 42 points.


His new tandem candidate, Toledo, has experience in the CEV event. Last season, Spaniard occupied the 10th position. SAG Racing Team hopes that the racer can bring the squad into the race for the JuniorGP Moto2 title.


“We are very pleased with the arrival of Alex Toledo to the team. He will receive all the support from our side so that he can show his full potential,” said SAG Team Manager, Edu Perales, in a written statement.


“Alex has joined the team's pre-season planning and has been training with our coach Josep Monge. He will soon train with Bo (Bendsneyder), Gabri (Rodrigo) and Piotr.


"From the team we wish you good luck this season, we are sure we will achieve many great results together!"


Will defend SAG Racing Team, Toledo did not cover his excitement. He felt so grateful, because he was offered a contract to join.


"This will be my first time in a big team. I really hope we can enjoy and achieve good results together."


"I'm really looking forward to starting working with the team and riding the bike. This is going to be a great year! A big thank you to Eva, Edu and all the sponsors who made this possible!”


Toledo's announcement itself raised questions about Dimas Ekky's future with the SAG Racing Team. Does this mean that the man from Depok is absent from the JuniorGP Moto2 championship.


Dimas is indeed a SAG Racing Team racer throughout CEV Moto2 2021. However, please note that he is under the contract of Stylobike, which is affiliated with Mandalika Racing Team Indonesia (MRTI).


Until now, there is no clarity regarding the fate of Dimas. Is the contract extended by MRTI or not. In addition, some time ago news emerged about the rights and obligations of the racer who had not been fulfilled by the team.
