Assen opens opportunity for Dennis Foggia to cut gap before the break


Dennis Foggia is set to take on the Dutch Moto3 this weekend, a race he won last season. So, the race at the Assen Circuit will be the perfect moment for him to close the first half of 2022.

By: I Gede Ardy Estrada, June 24, 2021, 12:08 AM

The Moto3 World Championship has completed 10 races this year. On 24-26 June 2022, the 11th round is scheduled to take place in Assen, Netherlands. It was the closing race before the summer break.


Leopard Racing's Dennis Foggia has good memories ahead of this weekend's Dutch Grand Prix. In the 2021 edition, he successfully grabbed the main podium at the Assen Circuit after a narrow win over GasGas Aspar Team rider Sergio Garcia, 0.078 seconds .


Foggia, who managed to finish second in Germany's Moto3 at the Sachsenring last weekend after failing in the previous five races , will of course aim for an even better result at Assen.


He has a chance for that. The Italian rider needed maximum results to catch up with the Aspar duo: Garcia (+51 points) and Izan Guevara (+44). So far, Foggia is in P3 standings with 115 points.


At the Sachsenring, Foggia was the only Honda rider who managed to make a difference with the NSF250RW bike. He clearly looks to want to reduce his deficit as much as possible before the five-week break.


“Finally a great result! It was a difficult race because even though I was faster in the first sector, in the last sector I was sliding too much in the fast corners," Foggia told Honda Racing's website.


“I could have risked more, but anyway, the smart decision was to cross the line to reach our goal, which is the podium. I really need to get back there.


"We've been very unlucky lately. So happy to be back on the podium. That's important after zero points at Mugello and Catalunya. Now we are going to Assen to get a lot of points. We want to fight again.”
